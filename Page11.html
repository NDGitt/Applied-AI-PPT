Key Takeaway:

Lean LaunchPad laid the foundation for rigorous, hypothesis-driven entrepreneurship. This course builds on that legacy—modernizing the approach with hands-on AI tools, structured MBA–Engineering collaboration, and a focus on deployable, real-world solutions.
